Context: Dungeon Master Invite Player to Campaign
Primary Flow:
	1. Invites player by username
	2. System find existing account with username
	3. Player recieves request
	4. Player accepts request
	5. Player is added to Campaign
	
Alternate Flow: Username not found in existing account
	1. System tells dungeon master no account found
	2. go back to #1
	
Alternate Flow: Player rejects invite
	1. Player reject inivite
	2. Player is not added to Campaign
